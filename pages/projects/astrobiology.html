Astrobiology - Plant the Moon

Astrobiology research support, presentation development, and interdisciplinary project work at RRCC.

Project Overview

This project focused on astrobiology and plant growth in space-related conditions through RRCC’s Plant the Moon work. It involved research support, scientific communication, collaborative project development, and helping present the work clearly and professionally.

What I Did

I contributed through project research support, presentation development, technical communication, and helping organize and explain the work clearly. I helped translate scientific material into a form that could be presented effectively to others while strengthening my experience in interdisciplinary collaboration.

Skills and Experience Built

This project helped build experience in collaborative research support, presentation design, technical writing, scientific communication, systems thinking, and interdisciplinary teamwork connecting biological questions with broader environmental and engineering contexts.

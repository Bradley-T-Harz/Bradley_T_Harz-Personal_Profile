Astrobiology - Plant the Moon

RRCC astrobiology project work involving research support, presentation development, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Project Overview

This project explored astrobiology and plant growth in space-related conditions through the RRCC Plant the Moon effort. The work centered on scientific communication, project development, and helping present the broader research clearly and professionally.

What I Did

I supported the project through research, technical communication, presentation preparation, interdisciplinary coordination, and helping shape how the project was explained and presented. I also strengthened skills in scientific storytelling, collaboration, and translating technical work into clear materials for broader audiences.

Skills and Experience Built

This project helped build experience in astrobiology communication, collaborative research support, presentation design, technical writing, systems thinking, and connecting biological questions with broader environmental and engineering contexts.
